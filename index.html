Biography

Zongwei Li is a Ph.D. Candidate in Cyberspace Security at Hainan University, supervised by Prof. Xiaoqi Li（李晓琦）. His research interests lie in blockchain security, smart contract vulnerability detection, and the application of AI in software security. He has published papers at top-tier conferences such as WWW and AAAI. His work has been recognized with the National Scholarship and multiple awards.
