Biography

Zongwei Li is a Master's student in Cyberspace Security at Hainan University, supervised by Prof. Xiaoqi Li（李晓琦）. His research focuses on smart contract security and blockchain technology, with publications in top conferences and journals including WWW and AAAI. He has received several honors including the National Scholarship and Outstanding Graduate Student award. Zongwei has participated in research projects funded by the CCF-Tencent Rhino-Bird Fund and the National Natural Science Foundation of China.
